Who we are ?

Kusum Ltd is a web development company from Croatia that creates awesome websites and ios apps.

We own HTML5 & CSS3 and always approach new projects with the same passion as the first website we built, it's fun for us, we like it and have high standards for ourselves which is very good for you.

Design is all about ideas and execution - two things less you have to worry about beacuse we have lots of ideas, sometimes crazy - sometimes normal and we execute at the top level.

Creating cool iPhone and iPad apps is not something new to us. From simple apps to hard-core games or services - we got you covered. Also we read Apple developer guidelines a gazillion times.

Custom CMS - no problem, we have made a lot of them and we can fulfill your every desire. Expect a user focused and friendly design, no bugs, no crashing - just works. Plus you don't need a tutorial to use it.
